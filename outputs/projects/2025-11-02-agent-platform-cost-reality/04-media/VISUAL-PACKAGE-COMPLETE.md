## DESIGN LEARNING: Why Hand-Drawn Works

**Research findings**:

- Engineers PREFER Excalidraw hand-drawn style over polished graphics
- Whiteboard aesthetic = approachable, focuses on ideas not polish
- Wobbly imperfect lines = human, relatable, not intimidating
- Casual handwriting = sketch-based thinking (how engineers actually work)

**What makes Excalidraw effective**:

1. **Removes perfectionism** - imperfect is the style
2. **Whiteboard realism** - looks like actual engineering discussions
3. **Approachable** - not corporate, not intimidating
4. **Focus on content** - ideas > aesthetics

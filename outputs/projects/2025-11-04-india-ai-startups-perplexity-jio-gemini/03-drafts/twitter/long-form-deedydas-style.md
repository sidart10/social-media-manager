## The Deals (All Within 7 Days)

**July 2025: Perplexity × Airtel**
- 360M users get Perplexity Pro free for 12 months
- Value: $200/year per user = $72B total
- Exclusive deal (blocks all other Indian telcos)
- Result: Perplexity hits #1 on iOS App Store in India

**October 30, 2025: Google × Jio**
- 500M+ users get Gemini AI Pro free for 18 months
- Value: $396 per user = $198B+ total
- Includes: Gemini 2.5 Pro, image/video generation, NotebookLM, 2TB storage
- Starting with 18-25 year olds, expanding to all Jio customers

**November 4, 2025: OpenAI**
- ChatGPT Go free for 1 year, nationwide
- No telecom partner (interesting)
- Announced 3 days after Google-Jio deal

Total giveaway: **$270B+ in premium AI services to 700M+ users.**

That's not market expansion. That's the **largest data acquisition play in history.**

## What India Has That Nobody Else Does

**700M+ internet users** generating:
- Multilingual queries (300+ languages, code-switching between Hindi/English)
- High AI adoption (50% of users already using some form of AI)
- Youth demographic (377M Gen Z spending $860B, projected $2T by 2035)
- Cultural diversity (training data for global edge cases)
- English proficiency at scale (largest English-speaking population outside US)

**Low internet costs** ($0.16/GB) = high experimentation.

**Digital infrastructure** (UPI, Aadhaar) = unique agentic AI opportunities.

Analysts are literally calling India **"the bot training capital of the world."**

## The Counter-Play: India's Homegrown Response

While US companies give away AI, Indian founders are building alternatives:

**Krutrim (Bhavish Aggarwal - Ola founder):**
- India's first AI unicorn ($1B valuation, January 2024)
- $1.2B committed by 2026
- 22 Indian languages, **20x more Indic tokens** than any global model
- Building India's largest supercomputer (end 2025)
- GB200 supercluster with NVIDIA (March 2025)

**Sarvam AI:**
- $41M from Lightspeed, Vinod Khosla
- 10 Indic languages, trained fully in India
- **4-6x faster inference** than larger models
- Open-sourcing models under government IndiaAI Mission

**Indian Government:**
- **$1.24B IndiaAI Mission** (announced March 2024)
- Building 10,000+ GPU sovereign infrastructure
- Supporting domestic LLM development

## The Real Game

Everyone's celebrating "democratization."

I see something different.

**Short-term:** India gets free AI → accelerates adoption → generates massive training dataset → improves global models

**Long-term:** India builds sovereign alternatives → Indic language moat → reduces dependency → owns the models trained on Indian data

Most countries pick one path. India is hedging both.

Google trains Gemini on Indian queries → deploys globally → sells back to Indian enterprises.

Krutrim trains on 20x Indic data → serves 600M Hindi speakers → Google can't compete there.

**They're not competing. They're coexisting.**

And the timing of all three deals within 7 days? That's not coordination.

That's competition.

**The Question Nobody's Asking:**

**What She's Doing Right:**

1. High-quality professional portfolio showcase
2. Selective posting (only shares best work)
3. Credits collaborators (builds professional goodwill)
4. Pins top achievement (film festival success)

**What's Missing (If Growth is Goal):**

1. Consistency - 11 posts in 5 years won't build audience
2. Captions - 50% empty = missed storytelling opportunities
3. Process content - Audience wants to see HOW you make this
4. Engagement - No response to comments, no conversation

**BUT:** If this is intentionally a **portfolio-only presence**, current strategy is fine.

### Recommendations (IF She Wants Growth)

**Immediate Changes:**

**1. Post Frequency**

- **More title sequences** - Top performer, show range

- **Achievement narratives** - "$500 budget, international festivals" = inspirational
